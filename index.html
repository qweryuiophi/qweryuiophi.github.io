Matarials

  • Toaster (dosn't need to be fancy)
  • Electricity
  • Bread
  • Eggs, Jam, Butter and etc
  • Plate
  • oven and oven tray If you can't buy an toaster
  • Butter knife if you are going to spread butter

    • These are the steps.

    First,put the toaster down and .Plug the electrical connector to the outlet.

    Next, put the bread into the toaster make sure to set the time! 3 minutes and 30 seconds should be good.

    Your bread is toast now!.

    • how to make a toast in the oven

  • Place the bread directly on the baking sheet.
  • Place the oven rack in the top third of the oven so it is closer to the heat sourse.
  • Take your toast in your pan and place it directly on the oven
  • close the door
  • Turn the heat on and after 30 seconds flip your toast and put it back in the oven for 40-50 seconds.
  • Take out the tray and you have toast!
