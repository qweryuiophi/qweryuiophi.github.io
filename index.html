Matarials

  1. Toaster (dosn't need to be fancy)
  2. Electricity
  3. Bread
  4. Eggs, Jam, Butter and etc
  5. Plate
  6. oven and oven tray If you can't buy an toaster
  7. Butter knife if you are going to spread butter

These are the steps.

First,put the toaster down and .Plug the electrical connector to the outlet.

Next, put the bread into the toaster make sure to set the time! 3 minutes and 30 seconds should be good.

Your bread is toast now!.

    how to make a toast in the oven

    1. Place the bread directly on the baking sheet.
    2. Place the oven rack in the top third of the oven so it is closer to the heat sourse.
    3. Take your toast in your pan and place it directly on the oven
    4. close the door
    5. Turn the heat on and after 30 seconds flip your toast and put it back in the oven for 40-50 seconds.
    6. Take out the tray and you have toast!

    It is perfect for spreading butter in my opinion.
